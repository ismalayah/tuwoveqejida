From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

First printed card by Takumi Wada

Takumi Wada (Japanese: 和田(わだ) 拓(たくみ) Wada Takumi; born 1983[1]) is an illustrator who has contributed artwork to the Pokémon Trading Card Game. For the Pokémon Trading Card Game: Online Illustration Exhibition, which was accessible between August 10 and October 22, 2022, they illustrated three different cards specifically for the online exhibition. These cards were never physically distributed. Their first card to be printed was the Aegislash included in the Paradox Rift expansion.

Besides working on the Pokémon TCG, Takumi Wada has been credited as the illustrator for The Legend of Zelda games Skyward Sword and Breath of the Wild.
