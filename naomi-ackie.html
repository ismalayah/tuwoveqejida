Naomi Ackie: Early Life, Singing in Whitney Houston Movie, Net Worth, and Personal Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Naomi Ackie, an accomplished English actress recognized for her contributions to both television and film, first stepped into the world of acting at the age of 11.

Since her debut, she has garnered acclaim for her performances in numerous well-received productions.

This piece explores Ackie’s early life, her involvement in the Whitney Houston movie’s musical aspects, and her net worth, and delves into aspects of her personal life.

Naomi Ackie Early Life

Naomi Sarah Ackie, born on November 2, 1992, in Walthamstow, London, England, hails from second-generation Grenadian immigrants.

Her father was employed by Transport for London, while her mother worked for the National Health Service.

Naomi, having both an older brother and sister, attended Walthamstow School for Girls.

Her journey into acting began at the age of 11 when she portrayed the angel Gabriel in a school nativity play.

Later, she pursued her studies at the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama, successfully graduating in 2012.

Did Naomi Ackie Sing In The Whitney Houston Movie?

In the biographical film “Whitney Houston: I Wanna Dance with Somebody,” Naomi Ackie took on the role of portraying Whitney Houston.

Despite her commendable singing abilities, the majority of the vocal performances in the movie are actually delivered by the iconic Whitney Houston.

Naomi’s singing voice was selectively utilized for specific moments requiring emotional resonance, such as certain poignant beats.

While she contributed to the gospel choir scenes at the outset, audiences will predominantly hear Whitney Houston’s voice throughout the film.

Is Naomi Ackie Lip Syncing?

Certainly! In the film “Whitney Houston: I Wanna Dance with Somebody,” Naomi Ackie is mimicking Whitney Houston’s stage presence and energy through lip-syncing.

However, it’s important to note that she is not actually performing the singing herself in the movie.

How Old Is Naomi Ackie?

Naomi Ackie, born on November 2, 1992, is currently 31 years old as of November 2023.

Who Sang In The Whitney Houston Movie?

In the 2015 biographical film “Whitney,” Canadian R&B singer Deborah Cox provides all of Houston’s vocals.

However, in the 2022 biographical musical film “Whitney Houston: I Wanna Dance with Somebody,” the majority of the singing is performed by Whitney Houston herself.

Naomi Ackie Net Worth

In 2022, the estimated net worth of Naomi Ackie is approximately $1 million.

Naomi Ackie Personal Life

No details are accessible regarding Naomi Ackie personal life as she has chosen to keep her private affairs out of the public spotlight.

Conclusion

Naomi Ackie, a skilled English actress, gained recognition for her performances in both television and film.

Making her acting debut at 11, she has participated in numerous acclaimed productions.

Notably, in the biographical film “Whitney Houston: I Wanna Dance with Somebody,” Ackie portrayed Whitney Houston, mimicking most of the featured songs.

Her estimated net worth stands at approximately $1 million, and she maintains a private personal life away from the public eye.
