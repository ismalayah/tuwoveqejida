Does Joe Rogan have kids?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Recently, people have steered their interest to know more about Joe Rogan, and by “knowing more,” we are talking about his personal life. It’s common knowledge that the UFC commentator has made a big name in sports, acquiring a position of one of the most influential personalities in the game, but fans have displayed curiosity to get more details of his family life.

While everyone is well aware of his marriage with Jessica Ditzel, not much is known about the couple’s kids. Rogan – who met Ditzel in 2001 – dated her for eight years before tying the knot in 2009, and she gave birth to their first child, a daughter named Lola, in 2008. Two years later, their second daughter Rosy arrived in 2010. As of 2023, they are 15 and 13 years old, respectively.

He also has a 26-year-old stepdaughter, Kayja Rose Rogan from his wife’s former partner Kevin Dono Connor, who is an aspiring musician and activist. Kayja has led many to look back at the life of Ditzel before she got married to Rogan, where started her career as a cocktail waitress before going on too dabble in modeling prior meeting Rogan.

Other than that, details about his daughters remain scarce, as Rogan has always kept his family life out of the spotlight. But that doesn’t mark the end of the discussion about his views on parenthood. The 55-year-old commentator and podcaster is a proud father to his golden retriever Marshall, who he introduced to his fans on Facebook by saying, “This is my cutie face Marshall. Golden retriever puppy and the sweetest little dog I’ve ever met.”

It is confirmed, then, that Rogan is a protective and laughing father to both human and canine, who is living a happy and blissful family life.
